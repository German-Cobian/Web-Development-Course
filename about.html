DesignWebtech Web Development Course 2021

The DesignWebtech Web Development Course brings together experts, academics and trend-setters from the global community of web-developers in a different country every year.

We welcome all aspiring web-designers to join us and partake of the vast pool of knowledge and expertise so as to further their goal of becoming integrated and to contribute their creativity to this thriving community.

Web-development is a thriving field, constantly expanding and whose possibilities are vast.

DesignWebtech Logo

We would like to acknowledge the contribution of Graphic Creators Inc. to DesignWebtech in having designed the log by which we present ourselves to the world at large.

The logo was decided by competition from among the submissions by participants of the many previous generations of DesignWebtech Web Development Courses.

We believe its slick lines, its contrast between neutral black and warm orange, and the pattern formed by two interwoven squares with rounded edges with eye-like circles at the center of their intersection neatly symbolizes the mix of technical complexity and artistic sensibility which characterizes web-design.
